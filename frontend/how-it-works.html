FAMILY SUBSCRIPTION

How to get added into a premium family or group account

1

STEP 1: Request to Join a family

Create an account on the site using your WhatsApp number and email. This allows you to submit a request to the administrator for your preferred family service. If you allow the site to send notifications, you will be notified when your request has been approved. If you do not have time to wait for approval, click the green button with the phone emoji on the bottom right corner of your screen to chat with our customer service on WhatsApp.

2

STEP 2:Payment verification(External for now)

Once your request has been approved, please complete your payment and send proof of payment via WhatsApp.

3

STEP 3: Subscription Activation

Once your payment has been verified, an invitation will be sent to you through the platform. Open the invitation, accept it, and add the address provided. After confirming your details, your subscription will be activated.

Who We Are?

Easycart Enterprise Limited Easycart Enterprise Limited is a small SME based in Zambia with a team of about four people, offering digital subscription services and technology products such as streaming access, digital services, and gift cards. The company operates mainly online through social media, digital marketing, and its website, allowing it to serve customers across urban and peri-urban areas nationwide. It targets mainly young adults and students, especially smartphone users who prefer assisted setup and flexible payments, while competing with both local resellers and direct subscription platforms on price, convenience, trust, and customer service. Easycart Enterprise Limited was founded in 2018 by a group of friends and is guided by core values of reliability, adaptive innovation, and efficiency. Its vision is to become a leading digital utility platform that supports how individuals and businesses manage their online needs, while its mission is to simplify the digital experience by offering instant access to essential subscriptions and tech services through a single, easy-to-use platform.
